Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our services, pricing, and process.

How long does it take to build a website?

+
A basic website is ready in 3–5 working days. A standard website with SEO is delivered in 7–10 days. For more complex projects, the timeline is discussed upfront before work begins.

Do I need to pay in advance?

+
Yes, a 50% advance is required before work begins. The remaining 50% is due upon delivery of the completed website. Payment is accepted via UPI, bank transfer, or cash (in-person).

Will the website be mobile-friendly?

+
Absolutely! Every website we build is fully mobile responsive — it works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Over 70% of visitors browse on mobile, so this is non-negotiable.

What about hosting and domain?

+
We recommend where to buy them and help with full setup. Typically a domain costs ₹700–1,200/year and hosting ₹3,000–5,000/year. We can also manage this on your behalf if needed.

What if I need changes to the website later?

+
The Standard package includes 1 month of free support after delivery. After that, small changes start from ₹500. An affordable monthly maintenance package is also available.

What exactly does social media management include?

+
10 posts per month (Canva graphics + captions + hashtags), 1 Reel, monthly performance report, and festival/occasion posts. You just approve the posts — we handle everything else.

Can you meet in person for project discussions?

+
Yes! We're based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. In-person meetings are always possible for clients in Lucknow. For clients outside Lucknow, everything is coordinated via WhatsApp and video calls.

Can you integrate online payment or booking on the website?

+
Yes, payment integrations like Razorpay, PayU, or UPI can be added. An appointment booking system can also be set up. A custom quote will be provided for these additions.

How will my website rank on Google?

+
The Standard package includes basic on-page SEO — title tags, meta descriptions, Google Search Console setup, and sitemap submission. We also offer Google Business Profile setup separately for local SEO.

Do you work with clients outside Lucknow?

+
Yes! Website and social media work can be done remotely for clients across India. Everything is coordinated over WhatsApp and video calls — no problem at all.

Can I see a sample before deciding?

+
Absolutely! Our Portfolio section has 6 types of live demo websites for different industries. After an initial discussion, we can also create a basic wireframe to show you the direction.

What is your refund policy?

+
The 50% advance is non-refundable if the client cancels after work has commenced. However, if we fail to deliver within the agreed timeline without communication, a full refund of the advance is provided.
